  • Which state in the life cycle of silkworm will be affected immediately if the mulberry bush dies suddenly? Clarify the reason.

    The larval stage in the life cycle of silkworm will be affected immediately if the mulberry bush dies suddenly. Because larva of silkworm is a varacious eater (feeder). It needes a lot of mulberry leaves as food and it dies in the absense of mulberry leaves.

  • Why are egges of silkmoth stored in cold during winter.

    The larva of silkmoth requirerd mulberry leaves as it is varacious feeder. After hatching of egg, larva of silkworm comes into the world but in winter, mulberry leaves are not available. So, to prevent the death of larva, egs of silkmoth are stored in cold during winter.

  • Why are purpae of silkmoth killed inside cocoon before extracting silk fibre?

    Pupae of silkworm are killed inside cacoon in order to obtain single unbroken silk fibre.

  • Why silkworm is called queen of fibre?

    The fibre produced by silkworm is shiny, long, attractive and durable. The silk fibre is the finest among all the other fibres. Therefore, silkworm is called queen of fibre.
