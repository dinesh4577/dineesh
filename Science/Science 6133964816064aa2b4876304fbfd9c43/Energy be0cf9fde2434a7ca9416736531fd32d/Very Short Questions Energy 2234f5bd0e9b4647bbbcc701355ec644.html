Very Short Questions: Energy

  • What is renewable source of energy?

    The type of energy source from which energy can be obtained continuously and can be used again and again is called renewable source of energy. E.g solar energy, wind energy etc.

  • What is the primary source of energy? Give one example

    The sources of energy which can be used in the same form in which they occur in nature are called primary source of energy. E.g wood, coal, dung etc.

  • Define nuclear reaction that takes place in the sun.

    The nuclear reaction that takes place in the sun in called nuclear fusion reaction.

    The reaction in which two nuclei of light atoms (like hydrogen) combine to form a heavy and more stable nucleus (like helium) with the liberation of a large amount of energy at very high temperature and pressure is called thermonuclear fusion reaction.
