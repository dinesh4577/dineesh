Force: Very Short Q&A

  • What is force? Write the SI units of force.

    Force is an external agency which changes or tends to change the state of body at rest or uniform motion, direction, shape etc. The SI unit of force in Newton (N).

  • What is Helio Centric Theory?

    In 1543 AD. Nicolaus Copernicus had formulated this theory

    It states that the sun is at the center of universe and all heavenly bodies like earth, moon, asteroids, etc revolve around the sun.

  • What is Geo-centric theory?

    It states that earth is at the center of universe and all heavenly bodies like sun, moon, other planets etc. revolve around the earth.

  • State the factors upon which gravitational force depends

    1. Square of distance between their centers.
  • What is gravitation or gravitation force.

    The force of attraction between any two bodies in the universe is called gravitational force.

  • What is the gravitational constant?

    Gravitational constant is the force of attraction existing between two unit masses (1kg) placed at a unit distance (1m) from their center.

    The falling of an object with an acceleration equal to acceleration due to gravity ‘g” in the absence of any external resistance is called free fall.
