  • What happens if an eggs is kept in two beakers containing pure water and saturated salt solution? Why?

    When an egg is kept is beaker containing pure water, the egg sinks because the upthrust due to water is less than weight of egg.

    When the same egg is kept in beaker containing concentrated salt solution, the egg floats because upthrust due to concentrated water is more or equal than weight of an egg.

  • An iron nail sinks in water but floats in mercury, why?

  • A ballon filled with hydrogen gas rises up but the baloon filled with air falls down. Give reason

    The density of hydrogen gas is less than that of air. So, the air exerts more upthrust on a ballon filled with hydrogen gas. Hence, the weight of displaced air is greater than the weight of ballon. So, ballon filled with hydrogen gas rises upwards.

    But the weight of ballon filled with air is more than the upthrust of air. So, ballon filled with air falls down.
