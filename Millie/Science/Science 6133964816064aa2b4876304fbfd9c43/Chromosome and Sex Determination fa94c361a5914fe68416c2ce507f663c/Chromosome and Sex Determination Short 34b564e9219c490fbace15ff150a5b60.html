  • What is meant by aneuploidy? Write with examples.

    The number of chromosomes may increase or decrease in daughter cells when the chromatids of mother cell does not divide properly. This process is called aneuploidy.

    Eg Down’s syndrome, Klinefelter’s syndrome, Turner’s syndrom etc.

  • Write down the major symptoms of Klinefelter’s syndrome.
    1. Growth of breasts in males.
    1. Infertility, tall stature.
    1. Small genitals, female type pubic hair pattern
    1. Poor beard growth, mildly impaired IQ etc.
  • What is meant by sex determination? Write in brief.

    During sexual reproduction, the process of formation of male or female organism during the fusion of male and female gametes is known as sex determination.

    Human beings have two types of chromosomes in each cell. They are autosomes and sex chromosomes. Autosomes develop physcial characteristics whereas sex chromosomes determines the sex of offspring.
