  • Justify with suitable chart that women is not responsible to give birth only daughters.

    Each somatic (diploid) cell of humab beings consist of 23 pairs chromosomes. Among them, 22 pairs are called automes and remaining one pair is called sex chromosomes.

    Each diploid cell of males has 22 pairs of automes and two types of sex chrosomes.i.e X and Y chromosomes. But each diploid cell of female has 22 pairs of autosomes and one pair of XX chromosomes

    In males during formating of gametes, gametes will have 50% X-chromosomse and 50% y-chromosome. But in females all gametes will have X-chromosomes.

    When the male gamete having X-chromosome fuses with female gamete, baby girlis produced. But when male gamete having y-chromosome fuses with female gamete, baby boy is produced.

    During fertilization, the formation of boy or a girl is 50%. So, it becomes clear that m ale and female have equal role to produce son or daughter. Therefore, we can say that woman is not responsible to give birth only daughters.

  • How is sex determined in human beings? Explain with the figure.

    Sex determination is the process of determining the gender of an individual as it begin life. Human beings have 46 chromosomes. Male has (44 autosomes + XY sex chromsomes) and female has (44 autosomes +XX sex chromosomes).

    As shown in above chart, during gamete formation male diploid cell divides into two type of sperms (22+X) and (22+Y) chromosomes and eggs having produced (22+X) chromosome only.

    When a Y-bearing male sperm fertilize the egg, the zygote has the 44+XY composition and the resulting embryo grows to boy. When an X-bearing male sperm fertilizes the egg, the zygote has the 44+XX composition and the resulting embryo grows into a girl.
